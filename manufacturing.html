Manufacturing Facility

The manufacturing facilities of Premium Atlas Chemical Industries are located at Dammam, Saudi Arabia. The operations are manned by a team of dedicated technical professionals who understand the importance of precise monitoring of process parameters for quality assurance.

Premium Atlas Chemical Industries guides user industries in selecting the right product from its range for the desired application, and provides guidelines for application methods and testing to ensure product efficacy.

For users of PAC Products for Fertilizers, it is necessary to take into account the following factors before deciding the economic level of dosing and the engineering method for dosing:

  • Designs
  • Granule/prill strength
  • Presence of fines
  • Moisture content
  • A host of other factors
