About Premium Atlas Chemical Industries Co.

Based in Dammam, Saudi Arabia, Premium Atlas Chemical Industries Co. is committed to local production of advanced chemical additives. Our work goes beyond performance—it’s about creating value for farmers, fertilizer producers, and energy companies by enabling higher crop yields, reduced environmental impact, and food‑grade quality assurance.

Every product we supply is guided by principles of sustainability: supporting industry transitions toward zero carbon emissions, minimizing raw material loss with zero wastage programs, providing safer, food‑grade solutions across the fertilizer chain, and enhancing resilience through local production.
